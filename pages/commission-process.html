The Commission Process

How Your Pet's Portrait Comes to Life

Creating a handpainted portrait of your beloved pet is a process I take seriously, with careful attention to detail at every step. Here's what you can expect when commissioning a portrait from Shelby's Pet Portraits:

1

Initial Consultation

After your order is placed, I'll review your submitted photo and any special notes or requests. Within 24-48 hours, I'll reach out to you via email to discuss any specific details, confirm your order, and answer any questions you may have.

2

Painting Process

I begin creating your portrait using professional-grade acrylic paints on high-quality canvas. This process typically takes 2-3 weeks, during which I carefully layer colors and textures to capture your pet's unique personality and spirit. I use archival materials to ensure your portrait lasts for generations.

3

Final Review

When the portrait is complete, I'll send you a high-resolution photo for final approval. If any minor adjustments are needed, I'll make them at this stage to ensure you're completely satisfied with the finished piece.

4

Delivery

After final approval, your portrait is carefully packaged to prevent damage during shipping. All portraits are shipped via insured courier service and typically arrive within 5-7 business days, depending on your location.

Typical Timeline

From order placement to delivery, the entire process typically takes 4-6 weeks. Rush orders may be available for an additional fee, subject to my current workload.

Materials & Techniques

I use only the highest quality materials in creating your pet's portrait:

  • Professional-grade acrylic paints for rich, vibrant colors
  • Archival quality stretched canvas
  • UV-protective varnish to prevent fading
  • Beautiful frame included with every portrait
  • Ready-to-hang with hardware already installed

Ready to Immortalize Your Pet?

I'd be honored to create a timeless keepsake of your beloved companion. If you have any questions about the commission process, please don't hesitate to reach out.
