Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of photo do I need to provide?

The best photos for portraits are well-lit, clear images that show your pet's face and expression. Front-facing photos typically work best as they capture your pet's unique personality. However, I can work with your favorite photos even if they aren't perfect. If you're unsure about your photo, feel free to send it over before ordering, and I'll let you know if it will work well for a portrait.

How long does the entire process take?

From order placement to delivery, the process typically takes 4-6 weeks. This includes the initial consultation, sketch approval, painting time, final review, and shipping. Rush orders may be available for an additional fee, depending on my current workload.

What if I'm not happy with the final portrait?

Your satisfaction is my top priority! I send a photo of the finished portrait for your approval before shipping. If any adjustments are needed, I'm happy to make them to ensure you're completely satisfied. In the rare event that you're not happy with the final portrait, I offer a satisfaction guarantee and will work with you to make it right.

Can you include multiple pets in one portrait?

Absolutely! I can create portraits featuring multiple pets together. The price depends on the portrait size and the number of pets. For each additional pet, there is a 50% surcharge of the base price. Please note that for multi-pet portraits, I recommend selecting a larger size to ensure each pet's details can be captured properly.

What medium do you use for your portraits?

I use professional-grade acrylic paints on high-quality stretched canvas. Once complete, each portrait is sealed with a UV-protective varnish to prevent fading and ensure longevity. The painting comes ready to hang with hardware already installed.

Can you create a portrait of a pet who has passed away?

Yes, many of my clients commission portraits as memorials for beloved pets who have passed. I understand how precious these memories are and approach these commissions with particular care and sensitivity. Even with older or lower-quality photos, I can often create a beautiful tribute to your companion. Please share any specific memories or personality traits that you'd like captured in the portrait.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, I ship worldwide! International shipping costs and delivery times vary depending on location. All international shipments are sent via tracked and insured courier services to ensure safe delivery. Please note that international customers may be responsible for any import duties or taxes imposed by their country.

Can I request a specific background or style?

Absolutely! I offer several customization options for your portrait. You can choose from:

  • Single color backgrounds in any shade you prefer
  • Beautiful border designs to frame your portrait
  • Your pet's name as an elegant add-on to the portrait

During the initial consultation, we'll discuss your preferences and I'll provide recommendations based on your photo and vision. Each customization option adds a unique touch to make your portrait truly one-of-a-kind.

Do you offer gift certificates?

Yes! Pet portraits make wonderful gifts, and I offer beautifully designed digital or physical gift certificates. These can be purchased for specific sizes or as a custom amount toward a commission. The recipient can then contact me to begin the commission process when they're ready.

Have Another Question?

If you didn't find the answer you're looking for, please don't hesitate to reach out. I'm happy to provide more information about the process, options, or anything else you'd like to know.
