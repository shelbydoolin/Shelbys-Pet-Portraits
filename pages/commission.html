Payment Process

After reviewing your request, I'll send you a detailed invoice with payment instructions. This allows me to ensure I can create your portrait exactly as you envision it.

Payment will be due upon receipt of the invoice, and I'll begin working on your portrait once payment is received.

What Happens Next?

  • You'll receive a confirmation email with your request details.
  • I'll review your photo and confirm it's suitable for a portrait.
  • I'll send you an invoice for the portrait.
  • Once payment is received, I'll begin your portrait.
  • You'll receive progress updates and a final photo for approval.
  • Your finished portrait will be carefully packaged and shipped to you.
